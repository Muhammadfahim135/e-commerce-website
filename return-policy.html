Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase

Last Updated: November 18, 2025

1. Overview

At eShop, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help. This Return & Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for returning products and receiving refunds.

Our Guarantee: We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be returned in their original condition with all packaging, tags, and accessories included.

2. Return Eligibility

Items Eligible for Return

You may return items that meet the following criteria:

  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • In original, unused condition
  • With all original tags, labels, and packaging
  • Include all accessories, manuals, and components
  • Not damaged or altered
  • With proof of purchase (order confirmation or receipt)

Non-Returnable Items

For health, safety, and hygiene reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Personal care and beauty products that have been opened
  • Intimate or sanitary goods
  • Perishable items (food, flowers, etc.)
  • Custom or personalized products
  • Downloadable software or digital products
  • Gift cards or vouchers
  • Sale or clearance items marked "Final Sale"
  • Items without original packaging or tags

Important: Electronics must be returned with all original accessories, cables, and documentation. Items missing components may be subject to a restocking fee or rejected.

3. How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to return your item:

1

Initiate Your Return

Log in to your account and go to "Order History". Select the item you wish to return and click "Request Return". You can also contact our customer service team at returns@eshop.com or call +1 (234) 567-890.

2

Receive Return Authorization

Within 24-48 hours, you'll receive a Return Authorization (RA) number via email along with return shipping instructions and a prepaid shipping label (if applicable).

3

Pack Your Item

Securely package the item in its original packaging. Include all accessories, manuals, and components. Place the RA number inside the package and attach the return label to the outside.

4

Ship Your Return

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location. Keep your tracking number for reference. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

5

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return (typically 3-5 business days), we'll process your refund within 5-10 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email when your refund is processed.

4. Return Shipping Costs

Free Returns

We provide free return shipping in the following cases:

  • Defective or damaged items
  • Incorrect items sent by mistake
  • Items that don't match the product description
  • Quality issues reported within 30 days

Customer-Paid Returns

For returns due to change of mind or buyer's remorse, customers are responsible for return shipping costs. Standard return shipping fees:

  • Domestic returns: $7.99 (deducted from refund)
  • International returns: Customer's responsibility

Free Return Shipping Program: Orders over $100 qualify for free return shipping within the US. Premium members enjoy free returns on all orders.

5. Refund Process

Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refunded within 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: Refunded within 3-5 business days
  • Store Credit: Issued immediately (optional)
  • Gift Cards: Refunded as store credit only

Refund Amount

Your refund will include:

  • Full product price (minus any discounts applied)
  • Original shipping costs (if item is defective or incorrect)
  • Taxes paid (if applicable)

The following will be deducted from your refund (if applicable):

  • Return shipping costs ($7.99 for standard returns)
  • Restocking fee (up to 15% for opened electronics)
  • Repair or cleaning costs (if item is damaged)

Note: Bank processing times may vary. Please allow 3-5 additional business days for the refund to appear in your account after we process it.

6. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size, color, or model:

  1. Return the original item for a refund
  2. Place a new order for the desired item

Fast Exchange Option

For premium members, we offer an expedited exchange service:

  • We'll ship the replacement item immediately upon receiving your return request
  • Your card will be authorized (not charged) for the new item
  • Once we receive your return, the authorization is released
  • If the return is not received within 30 days, your card will be charged

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

Immediate Steps

  • Take photos of the damage or defect
  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • Email photos to support@eshop.com
  • Include your order number and description of the issue

Resolution Options

We'll offer one of the following:

  • Full refund with free return shipping
  • Free replacement item with expedited shipping
  • Partial refund if you choose to keep the item
  • Repair or replacement (for applicable items)

Our Promise: We'll make it right! If you receive a damaged or defective item, we'll cover all return costs and ensure you get a replacement or full refund as quickly as possible.

8. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within the expected timeframe:

  1. Check your bank account or credit card statement again
  2. Contact your credit card company (processing can take time)
  3. Contact your bank (there may be processing delays)
  4. If you've done all of this and still haven't received your refund, contact us at refunds@eshop.com

9. International Returns

For international orders:

  • Returns must be made within 30 days of delivery
  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs
  • Items must clear customs inspection
  • Original customs fees and import duties are non-refundable
  • We recommend insured, trackable shipping methods
  • Refunds are issued in the original currency

10. Warranty Information

Many products come with manufacturer warranties. For warranty claims:

  • Contact the manufacturer directly for warranty service
  • Keep your original receipt and warranty documentation
  • Warranty periods vary by product and manufacturer
  • Some items may be eligible for extended warranty purchase

For assistance with warranty claims, contact our customer service team.

11. Return Address

Please do not send returns to the address on the original package. Use the return address provided in your Return Authorization email. The standard return address is:

12. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Return & Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.

Need Help with a Return?

Our customer service team is here to assist you with any questions or concerns about returns and refunds.
